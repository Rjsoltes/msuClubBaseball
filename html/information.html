Club Information

Dues: $240/year

League: NCBA (National Club Baseball Association)

Practice Time: Tuesday/Friday 9-11 am

Team Biography

The team was founded in 2010 by a group of Montclair State students, who did not think this could be done, but they put their heads together and the program has been running smoothly for the past 5 years. This program gives a chance to those Montclair State students who could not play on the Division 3 NCAA team to continue their baseball careers while at school. We currently play in the NCBA Division 2 District 1 South Conference and are always looking for new talent. Please do not hesitate to contact us.
